The Future of
Agri-Supply Chains

AgriChain is a decentralized platform connecting local farmers directly to warehouses and retailers. We ensure 100% transparency, quality assurance, and fair pricing through digital verification.

🚀 Core Mission

Eliminating Middlemen

By connecting farmers directly to buyers, we increase farmer profits by 20% and reduce consumer prices by 10%.

Real-time Tracking

Every quintal of crop is logged with a unique ID, allowing retailers to track the origin and storage conditions.

Quality Verification

Our admin panel ensures that only verified, high-quality A-grade crops reach the market listings.

⚙️ System Workflow

1
Farmer Registration

Farmers list their available stock with specialized emojis and quantity details.

2
Quality Audit

The warehouse team performs a physical check of the moisture and grade of the crop.

3
Admin Approval

The batch is accepted into the secure cloud database for public visibility.

4
Dynamic Listing

The crop automatically appears in the "Explore Market" section for buyers.

5
Secure Transaction

Buyers browse verified farmers and place bulk orders with full trust.

6
Data Integrity

Entries can only be modified by authorized personnel using secure API keys.

📈 Impact & Vision

Developed by CodexCrew, AgriChain aims to digitize the agricultural landscape of Maharashtra. We leverage modern web technologies to solve age-old supply chain problems, ensuring that farmers get the respect and price they deserve.
